Marc's website

Hi, I’m Marc — a mechanical engineering graduate with a passion for blending precision with creativity. This site is my digital workshop, where I bring my ideas to life through detailed CAD designs and dynamic browser games built with JavaScript. I’ve always loved the intersection of logic and artistry — from engineering concepts on the screen to interactive code that people can play and explore. Whether I’m sketching 3D models, coding a new game mechanic, or just experimenting with new tech, I’m constantly exploring ways to create, learn, and improve. This site is both a portfolio and a playground — feel free to browse my projects, test out a game, or reach out if you’d like to collaborate. Thanks for stopping by — and welcome to my creative corner of the web.
